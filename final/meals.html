3 Cheap & Easy Meal Prep Ideas

As a college student, I am constantly looking for ways to make my life easier (and cheaper). One thing I've found that helps immensely with my busy school and work schedule is preparing my meals for the week in advance so I can just grab one and go. I've searched through tons of different recipes and picked out my favorite cheap meal prep recipes to share with you!


#1 - Jerk Chicken with Pineapple Black Bean Salsa

$10.01 recipe - $2.50 serving

This is one of my favorite meal prep recipes! Coming in at a cool $2.50 per serving, this one is perfect for college students trying to eat healthy on a budget. This recipe is super simple, and can be served hot or cold!

Get the recipe!

#2 - Lemon Artichoke Quinoa Salad

$6.74 recipe - $1.69 serving

This recipe is perfect for storing in cute little glass jars. Light and bright (and only $1.69 a serving), this Lemon Artichoke Quinoa Salad is best eaten cold and keeps beautifully in the refridgerator.

Get the recipe!

#3 - Ground Turkey Stir Fry

$7.41 recipe - $1.48 serving

I absolutely love stir fry recipes, and this one is amazing because you can customize it to what you like! Make it spicy, mild, add noodles or extra vegetables - whatever you like! At $1.48 a serving, this recipe keeps best in the fridge so the vegetables don't get soggy.
